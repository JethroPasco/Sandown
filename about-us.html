Jeffreys Bay is a unique and unforgettable town, the home of world famous “Supertubes”, internationally recognised as one of the world’s premier surfing destinations. And Sandown Pet Centre is situated right in the heart of this surfers’ paradise.

The Pet Centre was established in 1985, when owner Mike Stanley, previously in the Restaurant and entertainment business, decided to look for pastures new, and relocated from Port Elizabeth.

Originally situated in the Sandown Complex, from where it got it’s name, the business started from humble beginnings, slowly developing to where it is today, one of the oldest established businesses in the town. In 2018 the Company was able to purchase it’s own property at 20 Goedehoop Street, opposite the local Primary School, where it continues to grow and thrive to this day.

The Pet Centre sells a large variety of exotic birds, from colourful finches to magnificent Macaws and Cockatoos! It also stocks both tropical and marine fish, as well as outdoor Koi and Goldfish. The reptile section houses Bearded Dragons and Geckos, while a selection of non venomous snakes are kept in the reptile section.

A large range of accessories are stocked for most pets, and the business houses a selection of outdoor garden features to complement the outdoor Pondfish.

A bustling Dog Grooming Parlour operates from the rear of the premises, with a fetch and delivery service available for interested clients.
