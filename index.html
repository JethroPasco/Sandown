Welcome to Sandown Pet Centre

Supplying Jeffreys Bay with pets, foods and accessories for over 30 years.

We are a well established Pet Store in Jeffreys Bay, specialising in exotic birds, tropical and Marine fish, and many other animals suitable for the pet industry.

Our Range

We stock a wide and assorted range of pet accessories, and we also have a large collection of water features and pond accessories for Goldfish, Koi, and other locally bred fish.

Dogs & Cats

Shop to find your cat or dog toys, accessories, foods, treats, or book your pet in for a groom to have them looking their best.
